About

Practice app for web development.

This app was made as part of web-development course at Metropolia uac. With main goal was to learn to use different open apis

Destination seeker is a photosearch service. Its main purpose is to help photographers to find good locations for their own shooting. After searching a photo based on a subject or a location, the user can see the location of the photo where it was made. Weather forecast for 5 days at this location and an easy one button navigation from current location. User can also see who made that photo and make a personal contact to the photographer by twitter or instagram if he wishes so.



api:s in use:


Unsplash
Open weathermap
Google maps js
Google geocoding
